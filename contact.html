Contact Me

If you have any questions, or if you would like to order a bag or purse please either message me through Instagram, or fill in the contact me form below. Please let me know which design your are interested in, and whether you would like any customisations. Since each design is limited edition, I cannot guarantee that I will have the exact bag you would like available, however if you let me know which shape, size and fabric you would like then I will do my best to complete your order.

If you are a fabric or accessory seller, and you would like to collaborate on any pieces then I would love to hear from you! I would like to support other small businesses as much as possible! You can check out my previous collaborations and read a bit about the amazing business I have worked with under the “previous work” tab on the homepage!
