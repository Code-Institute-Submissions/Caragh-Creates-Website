All my handbags and purses are unique and limited edition, and wherever possible I source my materials from other small businesses. I am more than happy to do custom designs if you have anything specific in mind! I would love it if you could take the time to have a little look through my work - all my bags can be seen in the gallery section, or on my social media accounts.

Since I make all the bags myself, and each design is limited, we do not currently have the capacity to take orders through the website. If you would like to order a bag or purse, please either use the “contact me” form, or message me on Instagram. While I cannot guarantee that an exact bag will be available, I’d you let me know the size, shape, and material that you are interested in the I will do my best to complete your order!
