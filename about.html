About Caragh

Having graduated in the midst of a pandemic, I started sewing by making a few items of clothing for myself, friends and family (some more successful than others) in the hope that it might cure a little lockdown fever. After creating my first Autumnal patchwork bag I really wanted to continue to seek out more beautiful fabric sets and experiment with designs, shapes and sizes of bag - but I couldn’t keep them all for myself! So I decided to start Caragh Creates in the hope that by selling some bags I might be able to keep creating new designs I potentially help support some other small businesses along the way!

Get in touch with me!
